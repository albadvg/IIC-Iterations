Management team

The Iberia Industry Capital Group is led by a team of international M&A, turnaround and restructuring experts.

Jeff Fenyo

Managing Partner

Mr. Fenyo is Managing Partner of IIC. He has an extensive track record supporting companies to restructure and return to profitability and many years of experience doing business in Spain. Previously he founded a turnaround consultancy and was a consultant at A.T. Kearney. He holds an MBA in Finance from the Wharton Business School. He is also a co-founder of Lansdowne Consulting, a leading international strategy and restructuring consultancy.

Tobias Hofmann

Managing Partner

Mr. Hofmann is Managing Partner of IIC. Previously he held partner and advisor roles for leading consultancies including A.T. Kearney in London, Paris and Munich. He has extensive experience in successfully turning around and restructuring companies on an international basis. He is also a co-founder of Lansdowne Consulting, a leading international strategy and restructuring consultancy.

Jan Pyttel

Managing Partner

Mr. Pyttel is Managing Partner of IIC. Before he was founder and board member of a major private equity and industrial restructuring firm. Previously he built up a group of textile-leasing companies. He has worked in the M&A departments for Lazard in Frankfurt and Salomon Smith Barney in London. Up until 1993 he acquired restructuring experience at the East German Privatization agency Treuhandanstalt.

Axel Folchert

Associate Partner & CRO Spain

Mr. Folchert is an entrepreneurial executive with extensive experience as CEO, CFO & CRO in leading national and international enterprises across multiple industries. He has successfully carried out strategic transformations, turnarounds and restructuring to bring under-performing companies back to profitability. He has more than 20 years of professional experience in Spain.

Romain Jeanjean

Associate Partner France

Mr. Jeanjean is Associate Partner of IIC. Previously he worked in the M&A departments of Lehman Brothers and HSBC and in the Real Estate Investment Banking team at JPMorgan in London. Romain has worked on numerous M&A transactions in Europe in a variety of sectors. He holds a Masters in Finance from the London Business School and an MBA degree from EM Lyon. He is also a partner at ARECAP, a corporate finance and real estate consultancy.

Transformation Executive
