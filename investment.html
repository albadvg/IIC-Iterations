Investment Focus

Our investment strategy is focused on businesses in special situations, such as strategic refocus of the seller, insufficient earnings or a lack of successors.

We are particularly active in supporting corporate divestitures and complex carve outs, in which sellers require a trusted partner to offer sensitive and long-term solutions.

Target investment opportunities will meet the following criteria:

  • Corporate divestiture or carve out
  • Minimum turnover >EUR 10 million or significant asset base
  • Marginal or negative EBITDA performance
  • Majority ownership can be acquired, with a preference for 100%
  • No specific industry, but focus on traditional, established product and service companies
  • Scope for operational improvement
  • National and/or international growth potential
  • Cost and revenue synergies with other portfolio companies

Please contact us if you have an opportunity that meets the above criteria.
